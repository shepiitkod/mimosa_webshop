About Us

🌼 MIMOSA

is the first flower to bloom in the South of France, announcing the return of light, warmth, and renewal. It is a powerful symbol of delicacy, resilience, and natural beauty. We believe that if something truly unique is to be created, it must be done with deep respect and care for nature and the environment that inspires us.

Our atelier

MIMOSA was founded in 2025 and is based in the very heart of beauty and fragrances — the South of France. Surrounded by sun, flowers, and timeless landscapes, we draw inspiration from the natural elegance of Provence, where craftsmanship and authenticity are a way of life.

What we create

MIMOSA specializes in the creation of three types of handmade candles:

  • Scented candles, designed to create atmosphere and emotion
  • Interior (sculptural) candles, created as decorative objects
  • Bento candles, perfect for birthdays, celebrations, and weddings

Our values

Each candle is made entirely by hand, with great attention to form, balance, and harmony. We use eco-friendly raw materials certified to European quality standards, and exclusive fragrances created in Provence.
